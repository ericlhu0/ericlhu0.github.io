About Me

Hey there! I'm a junior undergraduate student at Cornell University. I'm currently interested in human-robot interaction and safety using deep learning methods!

My academic journey is focused on developing algorithms and systems that enable robots to work safely alongside humans, particularly in caregiving and assistance scenarios. I'm constantly exploring new approaches to make robots more adaptive, safe, and personalized in their interactions.

I'm actively looking for research opportunities where I can contribute to robotics research. If you're working on related projects or have opportunities available, I'd love to connect!
